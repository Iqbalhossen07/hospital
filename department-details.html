Department of Cardiology

Leading-edge heart care with unmatched compassion.

Excellence in Cardiovascular Care

The Department of Cardiology at MediCare Plus is a center of excellence, dedicated to the diagnosis, treatment, and prevention of cardiovascular diseases. Our team of renowned cardiologists, surgeons, and nurses work collaboratively to provide a comprehensive range of services, from preventive care and diagnostics to the most complex surgical procedures. We are committed to a patient-first philosophy, ensuring you receive personalized care tailored to your unique needs.
