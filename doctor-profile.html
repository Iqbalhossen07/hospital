Professional Biography

Dr. Evelyn Reed believes in a holistic approach to cardiac care, combining clinical expertise with genuine compassion. Her journey in medicine began at Johns Hopkins University, and she has since dedicated her career to advancing the field of minimally invasive cardiac surgery. Dr. Reed is not just a doctor but a partner in her patients' health journeys, providing education and support every step of the way. Outside the hospital, she enjoys classical music and mentoring young medical students.
