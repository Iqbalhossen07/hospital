Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions? We have answers. Find quick solutions to common queries about our services.

You can easily book an appointment by clicking the "Book Appointment" button on our homepage. Then, select your desired doctor, choose an available time slot, and confirm your details. You will receive a confirmation via email and SMS.

We accept a wide range of major insurance plans. For a detailed list, please visit our 'Insurance Partners' page or contact our billing department directly. We recommend verifying your coverage with us before your appointment.

You can securely access your medical records, test results, and prescription history 24/7 through our online "Patient Portal." You can register for the portal at our front desk during your visit or by calling our support line.

For any life-threatening medical emergency, please call your local emergency number (e.g., 999) immediately or go to the nearest emergency room. Our hospital's emergency department is open 24/7 for urgent medical needs.

Yes, we offer telemedicine or virtual consultations for many non-emergency conditions. This allows you to speak with a healthcare professional from the comfort of your home. You can book a telemedicine appointment through our online portal.
