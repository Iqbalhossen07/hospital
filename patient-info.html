Admission Process

1

Doctor's Recommendation

Admission begins with a consultation and a formal recommendation from one of our specialists.

2

Visit Admission Desk

Proceed to our 24/7 admission desk with the doctor's recommendation and necessary documents.

3

Deposit & Paperwork

Our team will guide you through the paperwork and the initial deposit process.

4

Room Allotment

After completing formalities, you will be comfortably escorted to your allotted room or cabin.

Billing & Fees

Billing Process

We provide transparent billing with regular updates. An estimated bill can be provided on request. The final bill is prepared at the time of discharge. We accept cash, credit/debit cards, and mobile financial services.

Patient Counseling

A hospital stay can be a stressful experience for both patients and their families. At MediCare Plus, we offer professional counseling services to provide emotional and psychological support. Our dedicated counselors are here to help you navigate your treatment journey with peace of mind.

Services include pre-operative anxiety counseling, post-treatment support, and family guidance sessions.

Patient Rights & Responsibilities

We are committed to protecting your rights as a patient.

  • ✓You have the right to be treated with respect, dignity, and compassion.
  • ✓You have the right to be informed about your diagnosis, treatment options, and prognosis in a language you understand.
  • ✓You have the right to privacy and confidentiality of your medical records.
  • ✓You have the right to refuse treatment to the extent permitted by law.
