Utilizing state-of-the-art technology to provide crystal-clear insights into your health, ensuring accurate and timely diagnoses.

Our Diagnostic Technologies

MRI (3 Tesla)

High-field MRI for detailed images of soft tissues, joints, and the brain.

CT Scan (128-Slice)

Fast and precise cross-sectional images for detecting diseases and injuries.

Digital X-Ray

Low-radiation imaging for bones and tissues with instant results.

Frequently Asked Questions

For most MRI scans, no special preparation is needed. However, you may be asked to avoid eating or drinking for a few hours beforehand. Please remove all metal objects like jewelry. Our team will provide you with specific instructions when you book your appointment.

The scan itself is very quick, usually lasting only a few minutes. Including preparation time, your entire appointment may take between 30 to 60 minutes.
